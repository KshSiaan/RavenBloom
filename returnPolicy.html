Return policy

Return policy of Ravenbloom flowershop

At RavenBloom, we strive to provide high-quality products and services to our customers. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a return policy that allows you to return eligible items for a refund, exchange or store credit. This Return Policy outlines the terms and conditions of our return policy. Eligibility for Returns
To be eligible for a return, your item must be in its original condition and packaging, and you must have the original receipt or proof of purchase. Items that are not eligible for return include:


• Custom-made products
• Perishable goods, such as flowers and plants
• Gift cards

How to Initiate a Return
To initiate a return, please contact us at returns@ravenbloom.com within 7 days of receiving your item. Please provide your order number, the reason for the return, and whether you would like a refund, exchange or store credit.
We will provide you with instructions on how to return your item. You will be responsible for the cost of shipping the item back to us. Refunds
Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. The time it takes for your refund to be processed may vary depending on your payment provider. Exchanges
If you would like to exchange your item for a different size, color, or product, please contact us at exchanges@ravenbloom.com. We will provide you with instructions on how to return your original item and will send you the replacement item once we receive your returned item. Store Credit
If you choose to receive store credit instead of a refund, we will issue you a credit code that you can use on our website. Store credit is non-transferable and non-refundable. Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us at support@ravenbloom.com within 48 hours of receiving your item. Please provide your order number and photos of the damage or defect. We will provide you with instructions on how to return the item, and will send you a replacement or issue a refund once we receive your returned item. Changes to this Return Policy
We may update this Return Policy from time to time. The most current version of the policy will always be available on our website. We will notify you of any material changes to the policy by email or through our website. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Return Policy, please contact us at support@ravenbloom.com.
